About the Arabic Bible (SVD)

The Arabic SVD Bible (Smith & Van Dyck) was translated from the original languages by Drs. Eli Smith & Cornelius Van Dyck in 1865.

This implementation includes:

  • Complete Old and New Testament text
  • Word-by-word tagging with Strong's numbers
  • Links to Greek/Hebrew resources for deeper study
  • Parallel viewing with original language texts

Data digitization, formatting, and diacritics by "Arabic Bible Outreach Ministry" https://www.arabicbible.com/
